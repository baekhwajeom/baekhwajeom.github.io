About me

Hello! My name is Min Seo Kim, and I'm a mechanical engineer studying at Carnegie Mellon Univeristy for my Bachelor's and Master's degree. I enjoy bringing ideas to life by turning theoretical concepts into functional designs that I can tweak and test in real-time!

My current specializations are in 3D modeling, prototyping, and basic product design. However, I'm looking to expand from my current breadth of skills and continue honing others.

Recently, I’ve been diving into mechatronics — more specifically working with microcontrollers and basic embedded systems. I’m also learning more about simulation tools, particularly for finite element analysis (FEA), and I’m interested in deepening my understanding of heat transfer and thermal analysis to improve my design capabilities.
